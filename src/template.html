THE BADEGA

It's like a bodega, but worse!

Enter The Badega

The food is fire, the vibes are questionable, and the convenience store cat definitely doesn’t belong to us. Inspired by the chaotic charm of your neighborhood's most suspect corner store, Badega serves up late-night eats, unapologetic flavors, and a heavy dose of nostalgia. Whether you're here for a greasy-yet-glorious chopped cheese, a bodega-style breakfast sandwich, or just a tallboy and some attitude, we’ve got you covered. No frills, no fuss—just damn good food in a place that feels like home (if home had flickering neon lights and a broken ATM)
